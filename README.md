<h1 align="center">ComickSync</h1>

ComickSync is a simple browser extension that syncs your manga reading progress to Comick while you read on other sites.

## What it does

- Detects supported manga pages automatically
- Marks matching chapters as read on Comick
- Shows quick sync status while you browse
- Helps keep your Comick library progress up to date

## Site support

ComickSync uses MalSync adapter definitions for site detection and page matching.

That means ComickSync supports everything that MalSync supports.

## How to use it

1. Install the extension.
2. Sign in to your Comick account.
3. Open a manga chapter or series page on a supported site.
4. ComickSync will detect the page and sync your progress to Comick.

## Notes

- You need a Comick account for syncing.
- If a chapter cannot be matched automatically, you can retry or sync it manually from the extension UI.
